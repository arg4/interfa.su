Of course you can have two seats! How our service works is like this : first you need to pick the place you would like to visit, say you want to see Tokyo of 2980. We will get you to todays Tokyo and we will put you and your frend in a glass capsule. After that, we will begin the sequence (we will create a tiny black hole in front of the capsule so we can bend space, plus a few more technical sciency things that you don't have to know). What will hapen is, you will appear immediately in Tokyo (or whichever place you choose) of 2980 but you will be invisible for 30 minutes inside the capsule. That happens because we can't predict which place is occupied and because we don't want you to merge with another molecular entity (say a tree or a rock). Those 30 minutes of invisibility give you time to move with the captule controls in an unoccupied space so you can get out and explore. I hope that covers your answer, if you have any more, Dimi Kaye Time Travel Agency is here for you any time of the day (present day, past and future ;)﻿
